# ARCH Revenues — ICP (Current)

**Company type**: Founder-led agency (marketing, dev, Webflow, UI/UX, branding, product design, content, SEO — any B2B service agency)
**Revenue stage**: £/$/AU$ 10K–150K/mo gross revenue; founder still actively selling
**Headcount**: 2–25 employees; no full-time sales hire yet
**Geography**: US, UK, Canada, Australia, Western Europe (English-speaking buyers only)
**Buyer**: Founder/CEO — the person closing deals themselves today
**Growth pattern**: Revenue comes primarily from referrals and word-of-mouth; pipeline is unpredictable
**Buying triggers**: Just lost a big client; feast-or-famine cycle has gotten painful; tried cold email themselves and gave up; want to grow without hiring a full-time SDR
**Exclusions**: B2B SaaS products, consultancies without delivery teams, e-commerce, consumer apps, agencies with a dedicated sales team already in place

> This ICP was updated from the original v1 (B2B SaaS companies) to reflect the current positioning: outbound for founder-led agencies of all types.
