# ARCH Revenues — ICP v1

**Company type**: B2B SaaS company (subscription software, NOT services or consulting)
**Revenue stage**: $20K-$200K MRR (post-initial-traction, pre-Series A)
**Headcount**: 5-30 employees, with at least 1 founder still selling
**Geography**: US, UK, Canada, Australia, Western Europe (English-speaking buyers only)
**Buyer**: Founder/CEO or Head of Sales — the person doing outreach themselves today
**Tech stack**: Using HubSpot or Pipedrive CRM; Apollo or LeadIQ for data; LinkedIn Sales Navigator
**Buying triggers**: Recent seed/Series A funding (last 6 months); hiring SDR/BDR; launched new feature in last 30 days; raised prices in last 90 days
**Exclusions**: Agencies, consultancies, e-commerce, consumer apps, marketplaces, dev tools under $50 MRR
